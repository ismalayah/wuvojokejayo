Annie Wersching Husband Shared A Heartfelt Message On Her Demise

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  482 words  |  Martina Birk

Annie Wersching’s husband, Stephen Full, issued a heart-touching statement to CNN in tribute to his wife.

Annie Wersching, a well-known American actress best remembered for her role as Renee Walker in 24, passed away at 45 due to cancer. 

Wersching died in Los Angeles on a Sunday morning on January 29, 2023. Her publicist confirmed Wersching’s death to Deadline. 

Despite her cancer diagnosis in 2020, she remained active in her career and worked on projects such as Star Trek: Picard, The Rookie, and voiced the character of Tess in The Last of Us video game.

Who is Annie Wersching’s husband, Stephen Full?

Born in the Windy City of Chicago on November 13, 1969, Stephen Full was destined for a life in the spotlight. 

As of 2023, he is 53 years old, with a zodiac sign Scorpio. With a passion for acting, he stepped into the entertainment world in 1999 with his debut performance in Sons of Thunder as Jackson.

Over the years, Stephen’s talent and versatility have seen him grace many stages and screens, with memorable appearances in Morton, Heroes, CSI: NY, Resilience, The ½ Hour News Hour, Hannah Montana, and CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, to name a few.

However, it was in 2009 that Stephen achieved a career highlight with his standout role as Benny in the hit television show Castle. 

He continued to captivate audiences with his performances in I’m in the Band, Growing Up Fisher, Truth Be Told, Rosewood, Rizzoli & Isles, Santa Clarita Diet, 1-800-Hot-Nite, and many more. His career span has crossed over two decades.

In 2009, Stephen Full and Annie Wersching exchanged vows in a heartwarming ceremony at their Los Angeles abode, becoming one of Hollywood’s most beloved couples. 

Although the details of their love story remain a mystery, their bond is an undeniable testament to the power of true love.

As proud parents to three adorable boys, Freddie, Ozzie, and Archie, Stephen and Annie have created a warm and loving family. 

Freddie, the eldest of the three, was born on August 8, 2010, followed by Ozzie on August 4, 2013, and the youngest, Archie, on November 25, 2018.

Although Stephen is rarely seen in Annie’s social media posts and keeps a relatively low profile, he is a devoted and loving father to his three sons. 

In support of the family, a GoFundMe has been created to assist the boys in their future endeavors.
