American actress Maggie Wheeler has an estimated net worth of $2 million. She was born Margaret Emily Jakobson on August 7, 1961 in Manhattan, New York, USA, and is currently married to Daniel Wheeler, with whom she has with two children.

Wheeler began her professional career as a voice over artist on the animated series SilverHawks in the 1980s. She was also provided a voice on Batman: The Animated Series in 1997. She was also the voice behind Odile in Barbie of Swan Lake and Trinette in four episodes of Archer on FX. She appeared in the remake of Disney’s The Parent Trap as well as a guest star as Dina’s mom in the Disney Channel Original Series Shake it Up.

She then appeared as Janice Goralnik on the American sitcom Friends, which made her a household name, and later as Linda, Debra Barone’s friend on Everybody Loves Raymond. She had guest starring roles on shows like The Paper Chase, L.A. Law, Dream On, Civil Wars, Seinfeld, Doogie Howser, M.D. and The X-Files. She also had a recurring role on Ellen. Other credits included Will & Grace, Drake & Josh, The War at Home, Jack and Jill, Curb Your Enthusiasm, E.R., Friday Night Lights, How I Met Your Mother, The Sperm Donor and Without a Trace.
