Wiki/Biography

Helena Howard was born on Thursday, July 23, 1998, (age 22 years; as of 2021), in New York, United States. She grew up in New Jersey, United States. At the age of three, she became passionate about acting after watching the film The Mummy (1999). She studied at Union County Academy for Performing Arts, New Jersey. During her high school days, she participated in the Union County Teen Arts Festival, where she performed a monologue from David Harrower’s play ‘Blackbird.’ [1] Her performance caught the attention of Josephine Decker, a Hollywood director, who cast Helena in the film Madeline’s Madeline. Later, she pursued a course in acting at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, New York. [2]

Career

Film

In 2018, Helena Howard made her film debut with Madeline’s Madeline in which she played the protagonist Madeline, a lonely teenager who is part of a professional acting ensemble. The role of Madeline earned her overwhelming acclaim, and she was signed by WME agency, a renowned American holding company for talent.

OTT/ Web Series

In 2020, Helena Howard made her digital debut with the sci-fi drama series Don’t Look Deeper in which she played the role of Aisha, a teen human-robot.

Helena Howard in Don’t Look Deeper (2020)

Later, in the same year, she played one of the protagonists, Nora Reid, in Amazon Prime’s The Wilds. The series showcases the lives of a group of teen girls who must fight for survival while they are stuck on a deserted island after a plane crash.
